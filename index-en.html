Activities

Sankt Englmar

A state-recognized climatic spa and winter sports resort in the Bavarian Forest Nature Park. This mountain village offers both relaxing and active holidays in the beautiful low mountain landscape of the Bavarian Forest, in both summer and winter. Located near the fertile Danube lowlands yet deep in the mountains, between Predigtstuhl and Pröller, Sankt Englmar lies in a mountain pass of the Bavarian Forest ridge.

Predigtstuhl Resort

A family-style apartment complex consisting of eight interconnected buildings. Our Apartment Adriana is located in building 6 and Apartment Aneta in building 7.
The resort includes parking, an indoor children's play area, a football hall, an indoor go-kart track, a pool and sauna, a gym, outdoor playgrounds, sports and tennis courts, outdoor chess, a restaurant, a bar, and much more...
We look forward to welcoming you!

Summer

Inside the resort, all family members will find plenty of activities. There is an outdoor swimming pool, a large playground, a bar with refreshments, table tennis, outdoor chess, billiards, and mini golf.

The “Family World” is always available with outdoor and indoor activities, including climbing frames, slides, swings, electric cars, mini golf, pétanque, and more.

The resort also includes an indoor tennis and football hall, a climbing wall, and an archery range.

Indoors you will find a swimming pool, wellness area, and saunas.

The surrounding area offers cycling, mountain biking, hiking, Nordic walking, climbing, horse riding, fishing, and many unusual sports activities.

The longest summer toboggan run in Eastern Bavaria, bullcarting, forest climbing, a treetop trail, tandem paragliding, and numerous fitness trails provide fun for all ages.

Another summer attraction is BullCart – a downhill ride on a former ski lift track.

Golf lovers can enjoy nearby courses at Straubing and Deggendorf.

Sankt Englmar also offers natural bathing areas within walking distance of the resort.

Winter

The Predigtstuhl and Pröller mountains offer ideal conditions for winter sports lovers. Whether alpine skiing on 12 km of slopes, cross-country skiing on 70 km of trails, or snowboarding on prepared pistes – there is something for everyone.

Snowshoeing, sledding, skating, and curling are also available. Ski and snowboard equipment can be rented locally for the whole family.

The alpine ski area offers 12 slopes and 12 lifts.

The Predigtstuhl ski lift is located only 500 m from the resort and includes several lifts, including a 1100 m long double lift and the Herzerllift.

Kapellenberg offers fun for families and evening skiing under floodlights.

Grün/Maibrunn offers cross-country trails, a halfpipe, and floodlit slopes.

Pröller ski area provides family-friendly slopes, ski touring routes, and snowboarding opportunities with modern snowmaking systems ensuring reliable snow conditions.
